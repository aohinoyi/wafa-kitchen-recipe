Jollof Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Blended pepper & onions
  • Seasoning, salt
  • Vegetable oil

Method:

Fry the tomato blend, add seasonings, then stir in rice and water. Cook until soft.

Egusi Soup

Ingredients:

  • Egusi (melon seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • Vegetables
  • Stockfish or beef
  • Seasoning

Method:

Fry egusi in oil, add meat stock, season, and add vegetables. Simmer well.

Moi Moi

Ingredients:

  • Beans (peeled and blended)
  • Onions, peppers
  • Oil
  • Boiled eggs
  • Seasoning

Method:

Blend beans with pepper and onion, mix with oil and eggs, pour into containers, and steam.

Suya

Ingredients:

  • Beef slices
  • Suya spice
  • Groundnut oil
  • Salt

Method:

Marinate beef with spices and oil, skewer and grill over charcoal until done.
